Foundation Year in Science

Duration: 1 year
Level: Foundation

The Foundation Year in Science is designed for students who wish to prepare for undergraduate study in various scientific disciplines. This one-year program focuses on developing essential academic skills, including critical thinking, research methodologies, and effective communication. Students will engage in a curriculum that covers fundamental concepts in biology, chemistry, and physics, providing a solid foundation for further studies in science. By the end of the program, students will be equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in their chosen undergraduate programs.

Fee Type Amount
Tuition Fee (per year) $7,500
Application Fee $100
Student Services Fee $200
Health Insurance $500

Bachelor of Computer Science

Duration: 4 years
Degree: BSc

The Bachelor of Computer Science program offers a comprehensive education in programming, algorithms, and software engineering. Over four years, students will learn to design, develop, and implement software solutions that address real-world challenges. The curriculum includes hands-on projects, collaborative work, and exposure to the latest technologies in the field. Students will also explore topics such as data structures, web development, and artificial intelligence, preparing them for a successful career in the ever-evolving tech industry.

Bachelor of Business Administration

Duration: 4 years
Degree: BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program is designed to equip students with the leadership, management, and analytical skills necessary for a successful career in business. Over four years, students will study core business disciplines, including marketing, finance, human resources, and operations management. The program emphasizes practical experience through internships, case studies, and group projects, allowing students to apply their knowledge in real-world settings. Graduates will emerge as well-rounded professionals ready to tackle the challenges of the business world.

Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering

Duration: 4 years
Degree: BEng

The Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering program prepares students to design, analyze, and innovate mechanical systems across various industries. This four-year degree combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, covering topics such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, materials science, and robotics. Students will engage in hands-on projects, laboratory work, and internships, gaining valuable experience in the field. Graduates will be equipped to pursue careers in manufacturing, aerospace, automotive, and other engineering sectors.

Bachelor of Psychology

Duration: 4 years
Degree: BA

The Bachelor of Psychology program offers an in-depth exploration of human behavior, mental processes, and psychological research methods. Over four years, students will study various psychological theories and practices, including cognitive, developmental, and social psychology. The curriculum emphasizes research skills, critical thinking, and ethical considerations in psychology. Students will have opportunities for practical experience through internships and research projects, preparing them for careers in counseling, social work, human resources, and more.

Master of Data Science

Duration: 2 years
Degree: MSc

The Master of Data Science program is designed for individuals seeking to gain advanced skills in data analysis, machine learning, and big data technologies. Over two years, students will learn to extract insights from complex data sets and apply statistical methods to solve real-world problems. The curriculum includes coursework in data visualization, predictive modeling, and data mining, along with hands-on projects that simulate industry challenges. Graduates will be well-prepared for careers in data analytics, data engineering, and data-driven decision-making across various sectors.

Fee Type Amount
Tuition Fee (per year) $10,500
Application Fee $100

Health Insurance $500
